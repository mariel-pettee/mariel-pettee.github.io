I'm a PhD candidate in Physics at Yale University specializing in high-energy particle physics and researching the Higgs boson particle at the Large Hadron Collider at CERN.

I'm also a performing artist engaged in contemporary dance and theater.

Since 2017, I've led independent teams of researchers to generate choreography with AI models trained on my own movements.

CV

My thesis work with the ATLAS Experiment at CERN is the search for a rare interaction involving the Higgs boson decaying to a pair of tau particles.


I also implemented an RNN-based tau identification algorithm in the ATLAS high-level trigger software system.
